# Renovo Cape - Landing Site

## Project Overview

Build a modern, professional landing site for **Renovo Cape**, a restoration and enameling company based in the Western Cape, South Africa.

**Tagline:** "Restoration at its best!"

## Company Information

### About
Renovo Cape delivers quality products and service for restoration work. Their highly qualified team has over 60 years of combined experience, providing expert advice and premium results.

### Vision
- Quality Advice
- Quality Products
- Quality Service

### What They Offer
- Free no obligation quote
- Restoration works done on-site and off-site
- **Five-year warranty** on all re-enameling
- Coverage across the whole of the Western Cape

### Services

**Core Services:**
- Bath, Basin and Toilet Re-enameling
- Counter-Top Restoration
- Shower Trays and Tile Resurfacing
- Respraying of Kitchen and Bedroom Cupboards
- Epoxy Coating

**Specializations:**
- Chip Repairs
- Crack & Hole Refillings
- Polishing of Baths & Sinks
- Resurfacing of Appliances, Cupboards, Counter-Tops & Tiles
- Re-enameling of Baths, Jacuzzis, Urinals, Sinks & Shower Trays

**URL Format:**
```
https://wa.me/<number>?text=<urlencoded-prefilled-message>
```

**Renovo Cape WhatsApp:**
```
https://wa.me/27611990191?text=Hi%20Renovo%20Cape%2C%20I%27d%20like%20a%20free%20quote%20for%20restoration%20work.
```
